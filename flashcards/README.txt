FlashCards Study App - Data Directory
======================================

This directory contains your flashcard data:
- *.ser files: These are your flashcard modules
- images/: This folder contains all the images used in your flashcards

To share your flashcards with others:
1. Copy this entire 'flashcards' directory
2. Share it with the other person
3. They should place it in the same location as their FlashCards app

Note: Do not modify any files manually as this may corrupt your data.
